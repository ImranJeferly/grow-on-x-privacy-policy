Privacy Policy

Extension Name: Grow on X: Consistency Tracker

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Grow on X runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data to any external servers.

2. Data We Store (Locally)

This extension uses your browser's Local Storage (Chrome Storage API) to save:

  • Activity Counts: The number of posts and replies you make daily.
  • Goals: Your personal daily targets for replies and posts.
  • Cached Profile Info: Your X handle and avatar (retrieved locally from X.com to display in your dashboard).

This data never leaves your browser.

3. Permissions

We require specific permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your progress and history locally.
  • Host Permissions (x.com / twitter.com): To detect when you click "Reply" or "Post" buttons so we can increment your counter. We do not read the content of your posts.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or tracking pixels. The extension is 100% self-contained.
